So, a little more context about me. My name is Max Blaul. I am 25 years old and I'm from Evanston, IL, currently residing in Wilmette. Earlier that year, I joined the jam band Totem Frogs, playing bass guitar. I met the group through an app that facilitated networking between musicians. For the last few years I've been playing almost exclusively with the Frogs. We currently consist of 2 guitars, bass, drums, and a keyboard; played by Alex, Matt, myself, Matthew and Josh respectively. We've been together in this lineup since May of this year.
